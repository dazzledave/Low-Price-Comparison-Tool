About Pic2Price

Snap. Compare. Save. — Your AI-powered price comparison tool for smarter shopping in Ghana and beyond.

What is Pic2Price?

Pic2Price is an innovative platform that leverages artificial intelligence to help you find the best deals on products across top online stores like Jumia, Melcom, CompuGhana, and Amazon. Whether you have a product image or a name, Pic2Price makes price comparison effortless and accurate.

Image Search

Upload a product photo and let our AI recognize it, then instantly compare prices across multiple stores.

Text Search

Type a product name or keyword to quickly find and compare prices from leading retailers.

Wishlist

Save your favorite products and track their prices over time, all in one place.

Price Alerts

Set your target price and get notified when a product drops to your desired amount.

Analytics

View price trends, popular searches, and historical data to make informed buying decisions.

Modern UI

Enjoy a sleek, user-friendly interface with full dark mode support for comfortable browsing.

AI Models & Recognition

CLIP Model

We use OpenAI's CLIP (Contrastive Language–Image Pretraining) model for powerful image-to-text recognition, enabling users to search for products using photos with high accuracy.

Custom Classifiers

Custom-trained image classifiers further refine product recognition, ensuring relevant and precise search results for a wide range of products.

Services & Technologies

Python & Flask

The backend is built with Flask, a lightweight Python web framework, ensuring fast and reliable performance.

SQLite Database

All user data, search history, wishlists, and price alerts are securely stored using SQLite for simplicity and portability.

Web Scraping

Custom scrapers fetch real-time product data from Jumia, Melcom, CompuGhana, and Amazon, ensuring up-to-date price comparisons.

Currency Conversion

Automatic currency conversion lets you compare prices in your preferred currency, using up-to-date exchange rates.

Analytics Engine

Track price trends, popular searches, and product history with built-in analytics and data visualization tools.

System Architecture

Modular Design

Pic2Price is organized into modules for scraping, image recognition, data management, and user interaction, making it robust and maintainable.

Concurrent Scraping

Uses Python's ThreadPoolExecutor to scrape multiple stores in parallel, delivering fast results even for complex searches.

Security & Privacy

User data is handled securely, with privacy in mind. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Why Choose Pic2Price?

  • Save time and money by comparing prices from top retailers in one place.
  • Leverage AI for accurate product recognition and smart search results.
  • Track your favorite products and never miss a deal.
  • Enjoy a modern, user-friendly interface with dark mode support.

How It Works

  1. 1 Upload an image or enter a product name.
  2. 2 Let our AI recognize the product and search for it across multiple stores.
  3. 3 Compare prices and find the best deal instantly.
  4. 4 Add to wishlist or set a price alert to track future deals.

Our Mission

We aim to empower shoppers with transparent, up-to-date pricing information and AI-driven tools to make smarter purchasing decisions. Pic2Price is your trusted companion for finding the best value, every time you shop.
